Serpant's labyrinth

This is a pixel art game made in C++

The objective of the game is to move through the maze and step on the traps. After enough traps have been step on the snake dies.

The snake will travel through the maze and if it crosses your path you will take damage.

Mazes are always be unique everytime you play the game.
